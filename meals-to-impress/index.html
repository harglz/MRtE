Steak and chips

A classic meal, guaranteed to please. Steak always tastes best when seasoned with pepper and garlic. Optionally, add diced chilli peppers for added heat.

  • Ingredients
    Ingredient Quantity
    Sirloin steak 350 g
    Oven chips 500 g
  • Preparation
    While heating a buttered pan, trim the steak, leaving a little fat.
