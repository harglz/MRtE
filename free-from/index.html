Bacon, mushroom and courgette risotto

This hearty italian dish is free from with no change to the original recipe, and is very filling. Serves 4.

  • Ingredients
    Ingredient Quantity
    Arborio rice 400 g
    Onion, diced 1
    Back bacon, diced 250 g
    Courgettes, sliced 3
    Mushrooms, chopped 300 g
    Chicken/Veg stock 1 litre
    Olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Preparation
    Boil a kettle-full of water, while dicing the bacon and onion.
    Fry in a little oil until browned.
    Add the courgettes, and the rest of the oil.
    Make the stock (2 cubes per litre), and add with the rice.
    Place the mushrooms on top, making sure they are covered in the liquid.
    Put the lid on the pan and simmer until the rice softens - but it should still be al dente.
