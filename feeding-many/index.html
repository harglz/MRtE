Pepperoni cheese bake

This homely pasta bake will go far, without costing the earth.

  • Ingredients
    Ingredient Quantity
    Pasta (normally fusili) 100 g per person
    Ragu/Dolmio Tomato Sauce 500 ml per 4 persons
    Cheddar cheese 20 g per person
    Pepperami sticks 1 per 2 persons
    Back bacon 1 rasher per person
    Carrot 1 per 2 persons
  • Preparation
    Boil a panful of water, with a little salt and a splash of oil. Half cook the pasta - it should still have some bite. Chop the carrots into thin slices, dice bacon and pepperami. Strain pasta and pour into a casserole dish with the tomato sauce. Mix in carrot and meat. Grate the cheese on top and bake at 200°C for 20 minutes, or until the cheese bubbles.
