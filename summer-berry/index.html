Hampton Mess

A classic twist on Eton Mess, with delightful blueberry notes, blended with meringue and raspberry. For and extra-rich dessert, try using double cream, and whip until stiff.

  • Ingredients
    Ingredient Quantity
    Single cream 400 ml
    Raspberries 200 g
    Blueberries 100 g
    Meringue nests 4
  • Preparation
    In a large mixing bowl, whisk cream until stiff. Press juice from a small handful of mixed berries and mix in. Squeeze meringue nests between palms, allowing pieces to fall into bowl - this will create a range of different sized pieces. Add almost all of the berries and mix in.
  • Serve
    Serve in your choice of bowl, top with the spare berries.
